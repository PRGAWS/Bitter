What is Bittr

Bittr is an Belgian microblogging and social networking service on which users post and interact with messages known as "beets". Users can post and read. Users interact with Bittr through browser. The service is provided by Bittr, Inc., a corporation based in Anderlecht, Brussels, and has more than 25 offices around the world. Beets were originally restricted to 250 characters, but now there is no limit. Audio and video are still not allowed ... but picture is coming soon.

Bittr was created by Youssef Boubiba, Anant Van den Borg and Ismaël Saadi in March 2006 and launched in July of that year. By 2012, more than 100 million users posted 340 million beets a day. In 2013, it was one of the ten most-visited websites and has been described as "the SMS of the Internet".
